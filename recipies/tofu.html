Baked Tofu

Description

This baked tofu is delicious

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of Tofu
  • 1 bottle of Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Slice the tofu in perfect 1x1 squares
  2. Put the tofu in a plastic bag
  3. Pour soy sauce in the bag
  4. Let it freeze
  5. In a preheated oven put the tofu for 30 min
  6. Enjoy
